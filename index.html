PV-PP Agent Decision Layer

Primary GPT entry point for the Productive Value / Productive Power (PV-PP) Framework

This repository hosts the main custom GPT built around the PV-PP framework. It is intended as the first and most comprehensive entry point for understanding the model, exploring its logic, and applying it to real decision environments involving individuals, institutions, systems, and AI agents.

Where conventional models often reduce choices to a single score, the PV-PP framework examines governing domains, thresholds, corridors of viability, productive power, perceived productive power, regime shifts, and structured action selection. The result is a broader decision architecture designed to better explain how real systems succeed, fail, adapt, distort, or collapse under pressure.

What It Can Do

  • Explain the PV-PP framework in plain English or technical terms
  • Analyze decisions using thresholds, domains, and corridors
  • Compare PV-PP reasoning against scalar utility models
  • Interpret benchmarks and simulations
  • Explore AI governance, incentives, failure states, and recovery logic
  • Apply the framework to economics, addiction, institutions, and behavior

Why It Matters

Many systems appear rational until conditions change. A single-score optimizer may look effective while quietly damaging recovery capacity, resilience, trust, safety, or future options. PV-PP is designed to surface those hidden losses before collapse becomes visible.

This GPT serves as a working interface for that broader view.
